Career Aspirations 

Career Aspiration: AI Engineer

After ChatGPT, the digital age of technology has tremendously evolved within the past few years due to AI improving substantially. Keeping up with the latest trends of technology is essential due to the recurring changes that appear in the tech world. This time around, specifically generative AI has caught my interest, therefore I want to become an AI engineer.

Skills needed

Technical:

  • Programming languages: Proficiency in Python and familiarity with other languages like C# and Java.
  • AI and Machine Learning: Understanding of core concepts and practical application using current frameworks and cloud services.
  • Data Modelling and Engineering: Ability to manage large datasets, requiring proficiency in SQL and data processing tools.

Non-technical:

  • Communication: Clearly communicate complex AI concepts to stakeholders and work effectively with cross-functional teams.
  • Ethical Judgment: Grasp the importance of ethics in AI, including implications for privacy and bias.
  • Continuous Learning Mindset: Committed to lifelong learning and adaptability within a changing environment.
